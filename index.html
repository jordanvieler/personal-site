About Me

My name is Jordan Vieler. I graduated from the University of California Santa Barbara in 2020 with a B.S. in Cellular and Developmental Biology and a Certificate in Technology Management.

I grew up a year-round swimmer, transitioned to rowing boats in college and now train Brazillian Jiu Jitsu.

My interests include Neurobiology, Biotechnology, Artificial Intelligence, Computers, Business, and Entrepreneurship. I enjoy viewing ideas, concepts and problems through the lenses of seemingly disparate domains to find novel insights.

You can currently find me around Austin, Texas.
